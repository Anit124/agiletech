BJP OBC Morcha

Other Backward Class wing of the biggest political party of the world Bharatiya Janata Party.

Our current home minister and ex BJP President Shri Amit Shah announced BJP OBC Morcha, as seventh Morcha of BJP on 3 July 2015. The OBC Morcha aims to connect with the backward classes across the country and help create awareness of their rights, besides empowering them.In 2018 Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s government gave the constitutional status to the National Commission for Backward Classes (NCBC) despite the fact that there was resistance from the opposition parties. The National Commission for Backward Classes, which is a statutory body created in 1993, was given limited powers, only to recommend to the Government inclusion or exclusion of a community in the central list of the OBCs

The rise of the BJP

The rise of the BJP OBC front took place at the all India level in 2015 under the government of respected PM Narendra damodardas Modi ji. Bharatiya Janata Party took many historic decisions since 2015, in which the most important decision was the permanent structure of this organization, whose goal is vested politics, self-fulfillment, not the goal of becoming one-sided in politics.
